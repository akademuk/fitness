Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?
We are open 24/7 for members with keycard access. Staffed hours are 6am to 10pm daily.
Do you offer trial sessions?
Yes, we offer a complimentary one-day pass or a consultation with a trainer for prospective members.
Is parking available?
Yes, we have a dedicated private parking lot for our members with valet service available during peak hours.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months per year for travel or medical reasons.
